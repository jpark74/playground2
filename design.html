Little Red Riding Hood

Book cover illustration

This is my digital drawing project for publishing. I drew an illustration and created the title logo for the book named "Little Red Riding Hood". From the ideation and sketches, I was considered how can get the audience's attention without spoiler. I thought that giving a hint for the story but still in a curious way would perfect. So, I chose to place Red riding hood on the front side, it gets the audience's attention and makes them think where is she heading to. Audiences will begin to read the book with curiosity because of this, and it will make them more concentrated on the story. On the backside, I drew Grandma's house. When people have done reading, they will recognize that the house is the place where the girl was headed. I also created the title logo for the book. To match the overall feeling, I gave them curved tails and connected lines.

Breakie

Team Project / Typography / Type design / Poster design

We created a unique font for display typography. We created a sharp and broken feeling with triangular cuts for each character. It has sharply curved edges and tails.

Part of the project

I took the part of sketching upper case characters and converted them to vectors with Glyphs. By using Glyphs, I adjusted the kerning and tracking to make a perfect font to use. I created a typography poster to advertise the Breakie font. It is designing to represent the identity of the font.

 
Date: 2020, April
Used Program: Glyphs / Illustrator / Photoshop
Team member: Jungim Park (ME) / Lucette
